THE PROBLEM

The Regional Manager of R&S Supply reached out to me and expressed his frustration with the company's current website. Aside from its aesthetics, the site itself didn't offer what it should have. They haven’t updated their store directory in years and they now have an additional 20 stores in 6 different states, since its last update. They also don’t have a non-static logo image.

THE SOLUTION

The only logo that R&S had that was digital was a word document. I was able to recreate it into a vector image that would be reusable in future designs and merchandise. Since their color scheme was somewhat vibrant to use for the site’s website, I went with a flat design approach, which is a little easier on the eyes. Using the Google Maps API and custom-designed vector marker with their logo, I was able to successfully mark out all 23 if their stores on the homepage, as well as give contact information for each store.
